The Vikings began staying on after their raids against Ireland about half a century ago. Dyflin, the settlement by the Black Pool, soon became the most important of their well-defended costal bases, or longphorts, housing the largest slave-market in Britannia. Before succeeding his father to rule Dyflin five years ago, Bardr had already proved himself in numerous raids and skirmishes - proof positive that those who cross the Vikings of Dyflin can expect no mercy...<br><br>
