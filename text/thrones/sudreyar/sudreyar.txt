The islands off the west coast of Alba are numerous and sparsely populated. When the Vikings raided, and eventually settled here, they made little impact on the rest of Britannia. They called their new kingdom Sudreyar, the Southern Isles, to distinguish it from the island kingdoms further north, ruled by their kinsmen. Now, although Eirik's kingdom is diffuse and not materially rich, it provides a secure base from which to send out longships to raid or explore...<br><br>
