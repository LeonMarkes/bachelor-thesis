The kingdom of Mide lies at the heart of Ireland: not just geographically but politically, intellectually and spiritually. Many High Kings have come from Mide, and its current ruler, Flann, aspires to the title. Some say Flann's ambition makes him expedient, even ruthless, with his fondness for taking hostages and talk of dubious alliances. But with Ireland under threat from all sides, no one can deny that Flann is a king who will do whatever it takes...<br><br>
