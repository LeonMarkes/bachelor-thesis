Circenn is a land of misty hills and open moors, its folk a mixture of Gaels, Picts and others driven to the edges of Britannia by invaders. So far, however, they have resisted the Viking incursions. Scoan, the capital of Circenn, was once home to the legendary Stone of Destiny, said to bestow divine strength. Aed, Circenn's new king, will need all the strength and divine support he can get, not just against the Vikings, but to face potential threats closer to home...<br><br>
